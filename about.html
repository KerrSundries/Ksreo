About Kerr Sundries

Kerr Sundries is a unique emporium of curiosities, practical items, and whimsical treasures. Founded in 2024, we've been delighting customers with our artfull collection of sundries.

Our mission is to bring joy, utility, and a touch of the extraordinary to everyday life. Whether you're searching for that perfect artwork, a conversation-starting decoration, or a practical solution to a common problem, Kerr Sundries is your go-to destination.

Our Journey

2010

Kerr Sundries founded as a small corner shop in downtown.

2015

Expanded to include online sales, reaching customers nationwide.

2018

Launched our own line of Kerr-branded unique sundries.

2023

Celebrating over a decade of bringing curiosities to our valued customers!
